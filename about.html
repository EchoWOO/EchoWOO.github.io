About Me

Per aspera ad astra

The more you went through, the closer you are to the star

I am a passionate GIS analyst interested in data science, geospatial analysis and visual communication.



As the first dual degree student in Master of landscape architecture and urban spatial analytics ever in PennDesign, I am well equipped with abundant skills both on analysis, for instance, GIS, spatial analysis, data science, statistics, R and python programming and data visualization, and also graphics skills, landscape design and ecological planning. As an analyst and designer, I hope to make a difference to a field where I could combine my skills in both areas.



I have plenty of work experience as a landscape designer, architect and planner in various scales, phases, and types of projects, and also a researcher and analyst in multiple academic types of research and studies. These experience and education equipped me well in design and analysis abilities to articulate ideas, communicate with designers and analysts to contribute to group projects.



Through my diverse experiences and education, I understand the significance of combining spatial analysis and real-world research or design projects to contribute to decision making, project construction or making successful products. My future career ambition is to help organizations to optimize business decisions using my skills in spatial analysis, data science, and design.
