About Me

I am an experienced data analyst specialized in data science, geospatial analysis and visual communication.



With a dual master in geographic information science and urban planning, I am well equipped with proficient skills in spatial analysis, predictive modeling, statistical programming and data visualization.



During my three years of working experience, I have collaborated with multiple teams with diverse background on a span of very different projects in a fast-paced, enthusiastic environment. For example, I have worked closely with central business development, policy, data engineers and new market entry, as well as municipal staff across North America and Europe. My projects have a broad span from market size prediction to hardware dashboards implementation, also from assisting data migration to creating internal team websites.



I always have curiosity, passion and collaborative spirit to work in an innovative field and contribute my skills to optimizing business decisions or creating successful products.
